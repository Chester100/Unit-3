Unit 3 Final Project

Dilemma explained:

Your parent loses his/her job.You could help out by selling illegal dvds on the streets. What should you do?

Why you should do it:

Your family needs the money
So people can watch movies at home for cheaper instead of buying tickets at the theater
You would do anything for your family no matter the risk

Why you should not do it:

You could get caught
You could be charged and cause your family even more money issues. You could even be sentenced to prison for 1-10 years depending on what you download.
People may not want to buy the DVD's

My decision:

I would not sell DVD's illegally even if it does help my family because there are other ways to help out. For example, I could get a job or sell something that is legal. it is not worth the risk if there are other options that would be just as easy for me to do.
